About Us

The NESI Lab focuses on the design, synthesis, and application of nanomaterials for energy storage, energy conversion, and advanced sensing technologies.

Research Areas

  • Nanomaterials for chemical and biological sensing
  • Advanced spectroscopy for material characterization
  • Energy conversion and storage applications
  • Nanomanufacturing and device integration

Our Team

We are a multidisciplinary team of researchers, postdocs, and students working together to solve critical challenges in energy and sensing innovation.
